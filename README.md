## About

I research and build vision systems that work in the real world — across difficult sensor modalities, resource-constrained hardware, and complex unstructured environments.

**Computer Vision** — Expert in multimodal image fusion (Visible · Infrared · SAR) and end-to-end detection and segmentation pipelines. Developed novel SOTA-beating fusion algorithms spanning bi-modal and tri-modal synthesis, cross-attention architectures, and multi-layer feature alignment. Results published at IEEE TENCON, SPACE, and CONECCT.

**Deep Learning** — Design novel architectures with custom loss functions, training pipelines, and benchmarking frameworks. Apply knowledge distillation and model compression to achieve **3× faster inference** and **2× improved image quality metrics** over SOTA 2025–2026 fusion methods, with reduced model size.

**Edge Deployment** — Bring research to real-world operation by optimising and deploying trained DL models as **TensorRT engines on Nvidia Jetson**, with hands-on experience in the embedded constraints — memory, latency, and power — that govern real-time inference on resource-limited platforms.
